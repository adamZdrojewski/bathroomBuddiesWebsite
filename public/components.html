Appearance

Appearance is important to providing a positive and inviting atmosphere for guests to complete their objectives

How to get a perfect score?

Walls and floor are free of trash and stains. Trash cans are not full/overflowing. Units are clean, clear, and ready for use.

Atmosphere

Atmosphere is important to provide a comfortable experience for guests to complete their objectives

How to get a perfect score?

Bathroom has a pleasant smell, comfortable temperature, and appropriate lighting

Capacity

Capacity allows guests to complete their objective with minimal or no wait time, leading to a simplified unload and go process

How to get a perfect score?

An appropriate number of elements are provided for the demand (no wait time)

Layout

Layout is important to keeping the flow of guests moving in an efficient manner buy utilizing all elements

How to get a perfect score?

There is a proportional number of elements to each other. The layout is easy to understand and the units are well organized

Privacy

Privacy ensures that each guest does not encounter an unwelcomed guest while completing their objective at the unit

How to get a perfect score?

Stall doors lock, urinal walls pass eye contact test

Stocked

Keeping the elements stocked allows the guests to use the elements in an efficient manner and without disruption

How to get a perfect score?

Toilet paper, soap dispensers and paper towel dispensers (if provided) are all stocked

Exterior Presentation

Exterior Presentation is important to allow guests to find the bathroom easily and to present a welcoming environment

How to get a perfect score?

Bathroom is very easy to find (well-marked/labeled) and fits in to the surrounding area well

Theming

Theming is essential to providing a seamless transition from the outside of the bathroom to the inside

How to get a perfect score?

Theme is incorporated through the entirety of the bathroom. Theme matches the outside area

Uniqueness/Originality

Unique and original design choices give guests something positive to remember about their bathroom experience

How to get a perfect score?

Three or more unique or original design choices are present

Bathroom Buddies Definitions

  • Units - Toilets and urinals
  • Elements - Units, sinks, showers, soap dispensers, paper towel dispensers, hand dryers and trashcans
  • Unload and go (test) - The action of entering a bathroom, releasing fecal matter in a toilet, cleaning up, washing hands, and exiting without being interrupted
  • Clean, clear, and ready for use - The highest state of appearance for a unit and the unit is clear of waste, stains, and other imperfections
  • Eye contact test - Test performed while at a unit between two auditors. If eye contact is made, the test is failed
  • Objective - The reason the guest is visiting the bathroom. Can include, but is not limited to: release of urine, release of fecal matter and washing hands
